03Bookings & Payments

All bookings are confirmed only upon receipt of a signed agreement and the required advance payment as specified in the proposal. Payment terms, including deposit amounts, milestone payments, and final settlement, will be detailed in your individual service agreement.

  • Advance Payment: A non-refundable booking deposit is required to secure your event date.
  • Balance Payment: The remaining balance must be settled as per the timeline outlined in your agreement, typically no later than 7 days prior to the event.
  • Additional Costs: Any additional services or changes requested after the agreement is signed may incur extra charges, which will be communicated and agreed upon in advance.

04Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that circumstances may change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • 30+ days before the event: You may cancel with a forfeiture of the booking deposit. Any additional payments made will be refunded.
  • 15–30 days before the event: 50% of the total fee is payable, with the remainder refunded.
  • Less than 15 days before the event: The full event fee is non-refundable, as resources and vendors will have been committed.

Postponements are subject to date availability and may incur rescheduling fees. We will work with you to find the best possible solution.

05Client Responsibilities

To ensure the success of your event, we ask that you:

  • Respond to communications and approve proposals within the agreed timelines.
  • Ensure that the venue and any client-provided vendors comply with all applicable laws and safety regulations.
  • Inform us of any dietary restrictions, accessibility requirements, or special considerations for your guests.
